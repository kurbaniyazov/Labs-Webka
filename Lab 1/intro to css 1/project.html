Travel to Paris

Paris is the capital of France.Paris is one of the most beautiful cities in the world. It is known worldwide for the Louvre Museum, Notre-Dame cathedral, and the Eiffel tower.
It has a reputation of being a romantic and cultural city. The city is also known for its high-quality gastronomy and the terraces of its cafés.

More than 250 million people have visited the tower since it was completed in 1889. In 2015, there were 6.91 million visitors. The tower is the most-visited paid monument in the world. An average of 25,000 people ascend the tower every day which can result in long queues.

Sightseeings:

  • Eiffel tower
  • Musée du Louvre
  • Cathédrale Notre-Dame de Paris
  • Palais Garnier, Opéra National de Paris
  • Seine River Cruises
